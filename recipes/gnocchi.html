Gnocchi

Description

Pillowy gnocchi with a creamy mushroom sauce. Impressive your date with this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack gnocchi
  • 16 oz. mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3 cloves garlic

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook gnocchi according to package directions. Set aside.
  3. In a pan, turn heat to medium-high. Add butter until melted, then add mushrooms and garlic.
  4. Add heavy cream and reduce until thick.
  5. Serve sauce over gnocchi and enjoy!
