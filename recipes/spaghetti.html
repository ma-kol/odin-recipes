Spaghetti

Description

Delicious bowl of spaghetti. Tastes better than what you remember.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 2 1/2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 6 oz. tomato paste
  • 12 oz. spaghetti

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook ground beef until crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Cook pasta according to directions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a deep pan, mix beef and spaghetti pasta with tomato paste and chopped tomatoes.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
