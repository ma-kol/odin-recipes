Cauliflower Soup

Description

Hearty, homemade cauliflower soup. Great for rainy or cold days.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped
  • 1/2 cup salted butter, divided
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add half of the butter and all of the cauliflower.
  3. Transfer cauliflower into blender. Add chicken stock and heavy cream. Blend until smooth.
  4. Transfer mixture into a large bowl with other half of butter. Mix.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
